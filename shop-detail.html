About Our Store

At Fruitables sustainability is an integral part of our business model. Our goal is to build food solutions with the least possible impact on the environment. The results of our ISO 14040 compliant life cycle assessment (LCA) show that meals from Fruitables cause significantly lower carbon emissions than meals with ingredients purchased from supermarkets. In fact, on a global average, a Fruitables meal has 28% lower carbon emissions over the whole life cycle.

Our Goals

  • We change the way people eat forever.
  • Helping consumers to save money with every meal
  • Allowing everyone to enjoy a varied and tasty diet
  • Less food waste

    • Fresh and Locally Sourced

    At our store, we are passionate about providing you with the freshest fruits sourced locally. Our commitment to quality ensures that you get the best and most flavorful fruits for you and your family.

    Supporting Farmers and Communities

    By choosing our fresh fruits, you are supporting local farmers and communities. We believe in fostering relationships with farmers to bring you produce that is not only delicious but also contributes to the sustainability of local agriculture.

    Fruitables

    Eating fruit every day helps fight inflammation, prevent chronic diseases, enhance intestinal health, help rehydrate, beautify the skin and support weight loss. Besides the delicious taste and refreshing effect, fruit also contains many vitamins, minerals and fiber, making it a healthy food source that is easily found in daily life.

    Vegetables

    Vegetables contain the most vitamins and fiber. These are indispensable substances for our digestive system and body. Not only controlling fat and weight, vegetables and fruits also prevent many diseases such as arteriosclerosis, cancer, diabetes, blood pressure... You should eat vegetables in moderation. When eaten, it must be combined with other food sources to supplement nutrients.
